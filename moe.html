Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.

louvre museum

The Louvre Museum in Paris is one of the largest and most important museums in the world, featuring a rich collection of unique artworks from various civilizations and eras, including Leonardo da Vinci's "Mona Lisa" painting.

mont saint michel

Mont Saint-Michel in Normandy, France, is famous for its medieval castle and monastery, dating back to the 8th century. It's a popular pilgrimage site with a romantic atmosphere and stunning natural surroundings, attracting visitors from around the world.
