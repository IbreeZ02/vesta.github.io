Disneyland Resort Paris

Disneyland Resort Paris, or Euro Disney, is the largest amusement park in Europe. Suitable for children and adults alike. It is located in Paris, France, only 32 kilometers from the center of Paris in an area called Marne-la-Vallée. Disneyland Paris includes two theme parks in addition to seven hotels owned by Disney.
